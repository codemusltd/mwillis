Ferrophos

If a black satin finish is required this would suit. Ferrophos is also heat resistant with a good abrasive resistance. Being inexpensive it becomes ideal for a cosmetic finish on all those little jobs needing to look professional. One advantage is the article can be coated when assembled and will not lock up as with powder coat.

Parkerizing

A zinc phosphate coating giving a good corrosion resistant, as well as a good base for painting. There is a buildup of coating but it is crushable to an extent. The coat must be sealed by light oil or a laquer as it is slightly pouris in nature.

Molybond

A coating that has many applications. It is a dry lubrication which is bonded onto the surface of metal. The use of it on stainless steel bolts and nuts will stop the galling which saves cutting bolts to remove. Can be coated on steel, brass, aluminum and stainless steel. As a corrosion resistant standing 500 hours + in a salt spray makes it ideal for adverse conditions.
