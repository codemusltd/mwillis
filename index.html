Molybond
Molybond is a dry lubricant with many applications, which is bonded and coated onto the surface of metals. It's application, for example, on stainless steel bolts and nuts will stop the galling which saves cutting bolts during disassembly. Molybond can be coated on steel, brass, aluminum and stainless steel. Being corrosion-resistant, components coated with molybond are able to withstand 500+ hours of salt spray, making molybond ideal for adverse conditions.
